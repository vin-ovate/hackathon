Project Details :

Farmers often struggle to access markets, leading to lower income because
they rely on middlemen who take a significant cut. This problem limits their
ability to sell produce at fair prices. To address this, we gonna create a mobile
app that connects farmers directly with consumers and retailers.In which farmers
can list their produce, negotiate prices, and manage transactions, reducing the need
for intermediaries. The goal is to provide an easy-to-use platform that helps farmers
showcase their products, connect with buyers, and improve their income.
